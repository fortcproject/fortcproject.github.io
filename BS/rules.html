Battle Spirits Rules


Battle spirits have so many rules, the basic rules is hurt your enemy all heart. The card have 6 color and those have different special power.
Red-Higher BP and easily win in battle.
White--Higher defence and protect the heart.
Green--Gain more costs and summon more spirits in the battle.
Purple--Remove the enemy costs and kill the enemy easily.
Yellow--Use many magic card and turn around the battle.
And that's just one part of it, there are so many rules on battle spirits.
